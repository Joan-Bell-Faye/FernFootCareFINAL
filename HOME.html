What We Offer

Professionally Registered

Registered with the Health Care Professions Council, College of Foot Health Practitioners and Nursing and Midwifery Council. Provider of the most up-to-date clinical care.

Treatments

We offer a full range of foot care treatments and services for all ages. Each patient receives an individually tailored treatment plan for their needs.

Home Visits

Treatments provided in the comfort of your own home. We offer visits wherever you are, including rural locations.

Local Service

We cover the areas of Kirriemuir, Brechin, Forfar, and Dundee. We work with local GP's and other health providers.

Family & Friends

Appointments are offered on an individual or group basis for friends, families and those in care settings.

Fully Insured

All of our treatments and services are fully insured for your peace of mind.

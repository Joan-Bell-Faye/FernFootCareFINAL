Appointment Types

What is a routine appointment?

Covers all aspects of basic foot health including:

  • Nail Cutting
  • Ingrown Toenails
  • Nail Surgery
  • Callus & Corn Removal
  • Foot Wound Care

What is a new patient appointment?

First consultation, includes:

  • Medical history
  • Foot assessment
  • As required treatment
  • Callus & Corn Removal
  • Returning patients and 18 months or more since last treatment

What is a shared or group appointment?

More than one person for treatment at the appointment, includes:

  • Family & friends at same location
  • Care settings:
    • Sheltered accommodation
    • Residential care homes
    • Nursing homes
    • Workplaces
  • Discount applied, please enquire

What is an emergency appointment?

Appointments for high risk individuals, for example:

  • Diabetic patients
  • Infected wounds
  • Infected ingrown toenails
  • Impaired circulation
